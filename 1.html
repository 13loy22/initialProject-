The Postel Guesthouse- Cooliving




The Postelis a unique, affordable, urban shared living space located in Jaffa that hosts both short and long-term guests, so you will be joining a community of homes.

Just around the corner, you'll find antique & vintage shops by day, that turn into a vibrant nightlife area packed with bars and restaurants, at night. We are surrounded by delicious typical food & exotic markets. 2 min down the road awaits you the ancient biblical city of Jaffa & The Mediterranean beach with great surf spots.
